# Fixed-Time Running Data Collection

## Distances
- 100 miles
- 12 hour
- 24 hour
- 48 hour
- 72 hour
- 6 day

## Inclusion Criteria
- ~70% of world record or better
- Any other notable performances (historical significance, record holders, championship results)

## Data Sources (ranked by split richness)
1. **Timing company websites** — lap-by-lap or hourly splits
2. **DUV Ultramarathon Statistics** — duv.org, extensive results database
3. **Race-specific results pages** — individual race websites
4. **World Athletics / IAU** — championship results
5. **Articles, blogs, forums** — partial splits (hourly, intermediate checkpoints)
6. **Strava/GPS data** — where publicly available
7. **Books and publications** — historical performances
